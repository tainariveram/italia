Day Trip — Lake Como 🚂
Lake Como Day Trip
Plan
All groups · 8
One of the most beautiful places in Italy · boat between villages · effortless for grandparents and kids · quintessentially Italian
Full Day Plan
Full Day Plan
8:30 AM
Train Milan → Como
Trenitalia regional from Centrale · ~45 min · ~€5–8/person · runs every 30 min · no reservation needed
9:30 AM
Como Town — Coffee + Duomo
Walk the lakefront promenade · grab a coffee · explore the old town and the Duomo (free, 5 min, beautiful)
10:30 AM
🚢 Hop-on hop-off ferry to Bellagio
Buy day pass at ferry terminal · take the slow battello (not the hydrofoil) · ~1 hr with stops at Cernobbio, Tremezzo, Cadenabbia · sit on deck · ~€15–20/adult
11:30 AM – 1:00 PM
Bellagio 🌸
Most beautiful village on the lake · steep narrow alleyways · bougainvillea · views in every direction · gelato · genuinely not touristy once off the main strip
1:00 PM — Lunch 🍽️ Book ahead
Ristorante Bilacus, Bellagio
Terrace with lake views · traditional Como fish dishes · local pasta · not fancy · locals eat here · book 1 week ahead for terrace · group of 8
3:00 PM
⛴ Ferry to Varenna
15 min across the lake · quieter, more romantic village · walk the Via del Amore footpath · castle ruins with panoramic views (~€5/person, kids will love it)
5:00 PM
🚂 Train Varenna → Milan Centrale
Direct train ~1 hr · easier than going back to Como first · book on Trenitalia app
6:30 PM
Back in Milan
~€40–60 per person all in (train + ferry + lunch)
